Three steps

From blank page to draft.

1

Pick the document

Choose an NDA, MSA or DPA, then set your jurisdiction and — for the MSA — your industry.

2

Fill the details

Enter the parties and commercial terms. The agreement drafts itself live as you type, clauses adapting to every choice.

Download an editable Word file or save as PDF — then refine it and hand it to a lawyer for review before signing.

Important. Verbatra generates first drafts to save time, not legal advice. Every document should be reviewed by a qualified lawyer admitted in the relevant jurisdiction before it is signed. Using these tools does not create a lawyer–client relationship.
