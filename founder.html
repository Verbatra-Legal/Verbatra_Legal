Founder's Vision

Run your business. Leave the legal to us.

Verbatra is built for businesses that are just starting out, the way we did, and need their legal documentation handled without slowing down.

Business should be on the move. With a full legal team or without one, you still have agreements to sign, partners to onboard, and risks to keep off your back. That work cannot wait for you to be big enough to hire a department for it.

So I built Verbatra for the company that is small enough that cost genuinely matters, and ambitious enough that it is about to take off. The kind of business where every rupee has a job to do, and a full legal team is a cost you cannot justify yet, even though the legal work is already piling up.

Until you can bring us on full time, bring us on for the meantime. Run your business forward, and leave the legal operations to us.

That is the whole idea. You stay focused on building. We handle the contracts, the compliance, the documentation, and the quiet legal risks that sink young companies before they get going. And we do it for a fraction of what your own learned counsel would cost you. Think closer to one fifth.

Verbatra is the legal partner you can afford now, doing the work that protects what you are building, so that nothing about your legal house holds your business back.

, Arnav Bhardwaj, Founder
